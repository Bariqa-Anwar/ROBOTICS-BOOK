## Chapter 17 : Household Humanoids by 2030 – Roadmap & Predictions

### A New Dawn for an Old Dream

For over a century, the idea of a mechanical humanoid assistant has been a fixture of our collective imagination. From the "Tin Woodman" of Oz to Rosie the Robot of "The Jetsons," we have dreamed of machines in our own image.

Until now.

This book has been a journey through the technologies that are finally making this age-old dream a reality. We are standing at the precipice of a new era, the **Humanoid Decade (2025–2035)**.

#### 1. The Language Revolution : A Brain for the Body
The arrival of powerful Large Language Models has solved the greatest barrier to useful robots : the user interface. We no longer need to be programmers to use a robot.

#### 2. The Simulation Revolution : A Mind for the Body
High-fidelity simulators like Isaac Sim (Chapters 9 and 16) flip this paradigm. We can now generate billions of instances of experience in a virtual "matrix."

#### 4. The Hardware Revolution : A Body Worthy of the Mind
* **Edge AI Compute :** NVIDIA Jetson Orin (Chapter 10) allows the robot's "brain" to be small and efficient.
* **Actuation and Energy :** Advances in high-torque motors and battery density make robot limbs stronger and lighter.
